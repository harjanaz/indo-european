# Bres
§ Etymology: "The Beautiful"
§ "surrogate king [for Nuadu]...a curious choice since he was half-Fomorian..."
§ "His reign was not good, his niggardly ways caused Ireland's prosperity to fail."
§ "after the defeat of the Fomorians by the Tuatha Dé Danann, Bres was spared in return for his agreement to advise the Tuatha Dé on agricultural matters. [They]...were good at war and crafts but had not farming skills..."
