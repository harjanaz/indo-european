# Gododdin
## Trans.
### My
My Translation

I
Man's mettle, 
	youth's years,
	fearless in the fray;
Swift steed,
	thick mane,
	under bright lad's legs;
Blue blade,
	broad shield,
	strapped along side;
Shining spurs,
	fine furs,
	the twink doth ride.
