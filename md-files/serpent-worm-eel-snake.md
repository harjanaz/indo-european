## Etymology

- PIE "worm" `*u̯erm-`
	- Grk. `hrómos` ("wood worm")
	- Lat. `vermis` ("?")
	
- PIE `*He/ogᵘ̯ʰi-` ("snake, dragon")
	- Skr. `áhi-`
	- Grk. `ékʰis`

- PIE `*h₂engᵘ̯ʰi-` ("eel, snake")
	- Grk. `égkʰelus` ("eel")
	- Lat. `anguis` ("snake") `anguilla` ("eel")
