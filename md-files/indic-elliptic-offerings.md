## TO [god] THEE (epithet) (*svā́hā*) (verb)
|||
|-|-|-|

agnáye tvā svā́hā | To Agni thee, hail! | (TS)
agnáye tvā ā́yuṣmate | To Agni of the long life thee | (MS)
