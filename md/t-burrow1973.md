Nouns:
root + nominal-stem + optional-additional-nominal-stem
sró-t-as- ("stream")

A stem is made of a phoneme (the most common being: r, n, s, t, y/i, v/u, m, H, and k) and a possible preceeding thematic vowel (ex. -ar, -an, -as; IE er-, en-, es-).
