 ## Etymology
 - PIE `*peh₂-` originally "protect", later "shepherd/herd"
	- Skr. `pūṣan`
	- Grk. `pan`
	- Lat. `faunus` (?)
 	 - PIE `ph₂tḗr` ("father")
		 - PIE [[pie-sky-father]]
		 - Skt. `pitár`
		 - Grk. `patḗr`
		 - Lat. `pater`

A father, then, is the protector/head of the household (Lat. `pater familias`)
